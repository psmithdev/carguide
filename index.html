Used Car Guide: BMW E46 M3

  • VANOS Issues

    Problems with the variable valve timing system can lead to a lack of power at lower rpm, hesitant acceleration, rough idle, and long cranking of the cold engine. Fixing the entire unit with a rebuilt one may cost around $1800.

  • SMG Issues

    The Sequential Manual Gearbox can face issues like trouble shifting, jerks at lower speeds, and overheating. Replacing the entire gearbox can set you back around $7,000.

  • Subframe Cracks

    Cracks can appear in the subframe even on cars that haven't been raced. Repairing these cracks can be expensive, often costing several thousand dollars.

  • Rod Bearing Issues

    The rod bearings can wear out prematurely, leading to potential engine failure. It's recommended to replace them as a preventative measure, especially on higher mileage cars.

  • Valve Adjustments

    Regular valve adjustments are crucial for the S54 engine in the E46 M3. Neglecting this can lead to reduced performance and potential engine damage.

Used Car Guide: BMW E90 M3

  • Throttle Actuators

    The throttle actuators can fail, leading to reduced performance and a check engine light. Replacement is the most common solution.

  • Rod Bearings

    Like the E46, the E90 M3's rod bearings can wear out prematurely. Regular inspection and replacement are recommended.

  • Idle Control Valve

    The idle control valve can get clogged or fail, leading to rough idling or stalling. Cleaning or replacement can fix the issue.

Used Car Guide: C6 Corvette ZR1

  • Dash Peeling on the 3LZ

    The 3LZ trim, which is the highest luxury level for the C6 Corvette ZR1, has been reported to have issues with the leather dash peeling or delaminating. This can be due to heat, humidity, or just age.

  • Ceramic Brake Vibrations

    The C6 Corvette ZR1's ceramic brakes have been reported to cause vibrations during highway driving. This is attributed to the brakes not being balanced correctly from the factory that manufactured these ceramic brakes. The most effective solution is to have the ceramic brakes professionally balanced.

  • Supercharger Coupler Issues

    The supercharger coupler can wear out over time, leading to a rattling noise, especially at idle. Replacing the coupler is the most common fix.

  • Supercharger Bricks Need Reinforcement

    The supercharger on the ZR1 uses intercooler "bricks" to help cool the intake air. Over time, these can crack or fail, especially under high boost or when using aftermarket pulleys. Reinforcing or replacing the bricks with aftermarket solutions can prevent failure.

Used Car Guide: C6 Corvette Z06

  • Valve Drop Issue

    The LS7 engine in the C6 Z06 has been known to suffer from valve drop issues, particularly with the exhaust valves. This can lead to catastrophic engine failure.
